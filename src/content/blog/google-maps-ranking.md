# Google Maps Ranking for Contractors

## Overview

Google Maps often serves as the primary discovery channel for local service businesses. If a contractor business does not appear prominently in Maps results, configuration or technical issues may exist.

## Common Causes

### Incomplete Business Profile

Missing categories, descriptions, or contact information can affect visibility.

- [/insights/google-business-profile-issues/](/insights/google-business-profile-issues/)

Website indexing or performance problems may influence how your business appears in search results.

### Inconsistent Business Information

Differences in business details across directories may reduce trust signals.

## Quick Diagnosis Checklist

Check these items:

- search your business name in Google Maps
- confirm your business profile is verified
- verify business categories
- check that your website loads correctly
- confirm consistent business information online
