# Website Not Getting Leads

## Overview

If your website receives traffic but produces no inquiries, the cause is often technical rather than marketing-related. Hidden issues can prevent visitors from submitting forms, discovering your site in search results, or interacting with key contact elements.

Many businesses assume they need more advertising when the real issue is a malfunction within the website.

---

## Common Causes

### Broken Contact Forms

Forms may appear functional but fail silently due to configuration issues.

Common causes:

- SMTP email failures
- form plugin conflicts
- spam filtering errors
- server mail configuration problems

### Website Not Indexed in Google

If your pages are not indexed, potential customers cannot discover them.

Typical causes:

- robots.txt blocking search engines
- noindex tags on pages
- missing sitemap
- DNS misconfiguration

Related insights:

- [/insights/google-indexing-issues/](/insights/google-indexing-issues/)

---

### Website Performance Issues

Slow websites lose visitors before they reach your contact form.

Typical causes:

- large images
- server latency
- unoptimized scripts
- poor mobile performance

Related insights:

- [/insights/core-web-vitals/](/insights/core-web-vitals/)

## Quick Diagnosis Checklist

Check these items first:

- Test your contact form submission
- Search your website name in Google
- Test website speed on mobile
- confirm emails from forms are received
- verify the site loads without errors

If any of these fail, the issue may be preventing potential customers from contacting you.
