# Website Builders for Contractor Businesses

## Overview

Contractor businesses often rely on website builders to quickly launch marketing websites that showcase services, project galleries, and contact forms. These platforms typically combine hosting, templates, and editing tools into a single system. While builders simplify site creation, the level of technical control and support available varies significantly.

## Common Platforms Used by Contractors

Typical website builders used by small contracting firms include:

- WordPress with page builders
- Wix
- Squarespace
- Webflow

Each platform differs in how it handles hosting infrastructure, customization, and plugin support.

## Support Considerations

Contractors frequently require assistance with:

- updating service pages
- fixing contact form issues
- improving website speed
- troubleshooting hosting problems

Some platforms provide built-in support, while others require third-party developers.

## When Technical Support Is Required

Technical help may be needed when websites experience:

If your contractor website requires troubleshooting or updates, technical support may be required.
