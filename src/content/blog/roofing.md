# Roofing Website Problems

## Overview

Roofing contractors rely on search visibility and mobile accessibility. Technical issues may prevent customers from discovering the website or contacting the business.

---

## Common Website Problems for Roofers

### Mobile Performance Issues

Many roofing service searches occur on mobile devices. Slow loading pages can cause visitors to leave.

### Website Not Indexed

If roofing service pages are not indexed, they may not appear in search results.

### Contact Form Problems

Broken contact forms prevent potential customers from requesting estimates.

## Quick Diagnosis Checklist

Check these items:

- test contact form submissions
- search roofing services in Google
- check mobile page load time
- verify service pages appear in search results
