# Google Business Profile Not Showing

## Overview

A Google Business Profile is often the primary way local customers discover businesses. If the listing does not appear in search or Maps results, configuration or verification issues may exist.

---

## Common Causes

### Listing Not Verified

Unverified listings do not appear fully in search results.

---

### Category Configuration Issues

Incorrect business categories may prevent the listing from appearing for relevant searches.

---

### Address or Location Conflicts

Inconsistent location information may affect visibility.

---

### Website Technical Issues

If the linked website has indexing or technical problems, it may influence overall visibility.
