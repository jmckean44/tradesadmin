# Maps Listing Errors

## Overview

Maps listing errors occur when business information displayed in map platforms is inaccurate or inconsistent. Mapping services rely on structured business data and geographic coordinates to associate a business location with search results. Incorrect data can prevent a business from appearing correctly in map listings.

## Common Causes

- incorrect address formatting in the business listing
- mismatched NAP (Name, Address, Phone) information across directories
- map pin coordinates placed in the wrong geographic location
- duplicate listings representing the same business
- business categories configured incorrectly in the listing profile

## How the Problem Appears

- business appearing in the wrong location on the map
- map listing displaying an outdated address or phone number
- multiple listings for the same business in local search results
- users navigating to the wrong physical location

## How It Is Diagnosed

- reviewing the business listing inside the Google Business Profile dashboard
- verifying map pin placement using the address coordinates
- comparing NAP data between the website and map listings
- searching the business name to identify duplicate listings

## Typical Fix

- correct the address and map pin placement in the listing
- standardize NAP information across all business directories
- request removal or merging of duplicate listings
- verify business category settings within the listing
