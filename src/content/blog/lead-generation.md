# Contractor Website Lead Generation

## Overview

Contractor websites are often designed to showcase services but fail to generate inquiries. Lead generation problems frequently originate from technical issues that prevent visitors from contacting the business.

Insights library: [Technical Troubleshooting Articles](/insights/)

Homepage: [TradesAdmin](/)

---

## Common Causes

### Broken Contact Forms

Contact forms may appear functional but fail to send messages due to email configuration or plugin conflicts.

Related insight:

- [/insights/contact-form-not-sending-emails/](/insights/contact-form-not-sending-emails/)

---

### Poor Mobile Performance

A slow or difficult mobile experience often causes visitors to leave before contacting the business.

Related insight:

- [/insights/website-speed/](/insights/website-speed/)

---

### Website Not Indexed

If your website does not appear in search results, potential customers cannot find it.

Related insight:

- [/insights/google-indexing-issues/](/insights/google-indexing-issues/)

---

### Navigation or Structure Issues

Visitors may not easily locate contact information or service pages.

Related insight:

- [/insights/site-structure/](/insights/site-structure/)

---

## Quick Diagnosis Checklist

Check these items:

- test your contact form
- confirm contact information is visible
- verify your website appears in Google search
- test website speed on mobile
- check that service pages load correctly
