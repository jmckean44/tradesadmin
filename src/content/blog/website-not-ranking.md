# Website Not Ranking

## Overview

A website may fail to rank in search engines due to technical, content, or authority issues. Understanding the root causes is essential for restoring visibility and organic traffic.

## Common Causes

- Noindex tags or robots.txt blocking search engines
- Lack of quality backlinks
- Thin or duplicate content
- Poor site structure or navigation
- Manual penalties from search engines

## How the Problem Appears

- Website does not appear for branded or target keywords
- Search Console shows no impressions or clicks
- Pages are not indexed or quickly drop from the index
- Competitors outrank the site for all relevant queries

## How It Is Diagnosed

- Checking for noindex tags and robots.txt rules
- Reviewing Search Console for coverage and manual actions
- Auditing content quality and uniqueness
- Analyzing backlink profile and authority
- Inspecting site structure and internal linking

## Typical Fix

- Remove noindex tags and allow crawling in robots.txt
- Improve content quality and add missing pages
- Build high-quality backlinks
- Fix technical SEO issues and site structure
- Submit pages for indexing in Search Console
