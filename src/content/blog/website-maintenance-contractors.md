# Website Maintenance for Contractors

## Overview

Contractor websites require regular maintenance to ensure that software updates, plugins, and hosting environments remain compatible. Without maintenance, websites may gradually develop security vulnerabilities or performance issues.

## Typical Maintenance Tasks

Routine maintenance includes:

- updating CMS software
- installing security patches
- monitoring backups
- checking site performance

## Risks of Neglecting Maintenance

Without regular updates, websites may experience:

- [Website Security](/insights/website-security/)
- [Plugin Conflicts](/insights/plugin-conflicts/)
- [Backup Problems](/insights/backup-problems/)

## Technical Website Support

If your contractor website requires maintenance or troubleshooting, technical assistance may be required.
